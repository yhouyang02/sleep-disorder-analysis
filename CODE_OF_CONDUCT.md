# Group Project Code of Conduct

## Diversity and Inclusivity Statement

We pledge to collaborate without regard to age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual orientation. We work in harmony to foster an open and welcoming environment.

## Behavioral Standards

**Expected Behavior** To ensure a productive environment, we agree to:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Show empathy towards other members.

<span style="color:red">**Unacceptable Behavior** Harassment and exclusionary behavior will not be tolerated. </span>  This includes:

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- The use of sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Excluding members from decision-making processes maliciously.

## Consequences

If a group member violates this Code of Conduct, the following steps will be taken depending on the severity of the violation:

1. **Correction:** A private, non-judgmental conversation clarifying the standard and why the behavior was inappropriate.
2. **Warning:** A formal written warning denoting that continued behavior will result in escalation.
3. **Expulsion/Escalation:** A formal complaint raised to the Instructor (Sky Sheng).

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, depending on the severity of the behavior, raise the issue to fellow team members or to the lab Instructor/TAs.

- All reports will be kept confidential.
- The designated reviewer is obligated to investigate and respond to the report within a reasonable timeframe.
- Those responsible for enforcement will prioritize the safety of the person reporting the incident.
